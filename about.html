WANNA KNOW MORE I SEE

I've been composing for a number of years now and it's always for video games or anime. I listen to a LOT of music when I'm working on things and I believe that has helped me create these types of pieces. My ideas come from a lot of places. From ideas I recorded on my phone, in the shower and sometimes on the spot. I take those ideas and develop them into full pieces which is the fun and enjoyment that comes from it.
